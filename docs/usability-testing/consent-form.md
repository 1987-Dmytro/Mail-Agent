## Purpose of the Study

You are being asked to participate in a usability testing session for Mail Agent, an email organization tool. The purpose of this study is to evaluate how easy and intuitive the onboarding (setup) process is for new users. Your feedback will help us improve the product for all users.

## What You Will Do

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to:

1. Complete the Mail Agent setup process using your own Gmail and Telegram accounts
2. Think aloud as you work through the setup steps (approximately 10-15 minutes)
3. Answer questions about your experience (approximately 5-10 minutes)
4. Complete a brief usability questionnaire (System Usability Scale)

**Total Time Commitment:** Approximately 30 minutes

## Risks and Benefits

**Potential Risks:**
- Minimal risk. You will be using your own email and Telegram accounts in a secure, controlled environment.
- If at any point you feel uncomfortable, you may stop the session without penalty.

**Potential Benefits:**
- You will gain early access to Mail Agent and contribute to improving a product you may use.
- [If applicable] You will receive [compensation details: gift card, payment, etc.]

## Privacy and Data Protection (GDPR Compliance)

### Data We Will Collect
- Screen recording of your interaction with Mail Agent (no personal emails will be visible)
- Written notes about your actions, comments, and feedback
- Audio recording of your spoken comments (optional)
- Answers to post-test questions
- System Usability Scale (SUS) questionnaire responses

### How We Will Use Your Data
- To identify usability issues in the Mail Agent onboarding process
- To improve the design and functionality of Mail Agent
- To generate aggregate statistics (e.g., average completion time, success rate)
- To create anonymized reports for internal product development

### Data Protection Measures
- All data will be **anonymized** using a Participant ID (e.g., P001)
- Your name, email address, and other personally identifiable information (PII) will **not** be linked to your test data
- Screen recordings will be edited to blur any sensitive information
- Data will be stored securely on encrypted devices
- Only authorized team members will have access to the data
- Data will be retained for [specify period, e.g., 12 months] and then permanently deleted
